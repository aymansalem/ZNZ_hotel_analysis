ZNZ Hotel Booking Analysis

?? Data-driven insights from Zanzibar hotel bookings using Python and Google Colab

Overview Seasonality Occupancy Market Segments Conclusion

?? Overview

This project analyzes booking trends, occupancy patterns, and market segment dynamics for hotels in Zanzibar (ZNZ) using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn).

?? Seasonality Trends

ADR and occupancy rates across different months and years are analyzed to determine peak, shoulder, and low seasons.

?? Occupancy Analysis

Room Sold, Room Available, Occupancy %, and RevPAR calculated and visualized across properties and booking windows.

?? Market Segment Performance

Booking pace, channel mix, and nationality split are reviewed for FIT, OTA, Direct, and Corporate segments.

?? Conclusion

This analysis helps identify demand patterns and optimize revenue strategy by leveraging historical booking behavior.
